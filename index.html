What is JKT48?

We want to create a place for Indonesian women to fulfill their dreams. Together with the fans, we want to create the only "Original Indonesian Idol". This is our main inspiration for launching JKT48. All JKT48 members will strive to reach their goal of becoming true idols. For that, they will be highly dedicated to their activities. Not only singing and dancing, but also talent or other appearances. Come on, come and watch the members fight hard to realize their dreams. AKB48 can be successful because it grows "with" the fans so they can reach the position they are today. We want JKT48 to follow AKB48's footsteps to become an idol group that is close to its fans and also has a close relationship with them. We believe that the support from fans will bring JKT48 to a higher level, starting from an idol group based in Jakarta to becoming known worldwide.
